Why Shiftgig On Demand

Shiftgig On Demand is a powerful technology-driven service that can fill your seasonal, temporary, or contingent labor in minutes—wherever and whenever you need staff. From recurring shifts to one-off events, we’ve got you covered.

Service

The number one reason our clients keep coming back is our service. From full color rosters, hands-on field supervisors, special worker programs, a comprehensive worker mobile app, and our proactive account team, we ensure that everything runs smoothly by staying involved throughout the entire process. Don’t take our word for it—check out what our customers are saying.

Technology

Our proprietary On Demand technology simplifies the process of pairing qualified workers to client needs. Via an automated matching process, we eliminate the challenges associated with traditional staffing services, including scheduling and disbursing payment. Thanks to checkpoints built into our technology, we can ensure that hassles such as no-call, no-shows are a thing of the past.

Quality

Rest assured your work orders will be filled with reliable, competent, and friendly people. All of our workers pass an extensive vetting process, including phone screenings, background checks, drug tests, and skills assessments. We secure workers whose skills fit the specific requirements of your shifts, and who will show up on time and ready to work.

People

Drawing from our Shiftgig.com community of over one million job seekers, you can be sure we’ll find the right people for the job. From bartenders and servers to cooks and cashiers, we've got people ready to work all positions, including call center, warehouse, administrative, brand ambassadors, event registrations and promotional models.
