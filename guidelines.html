Community Guidelines

Shiftgig On Demand lets you find work you want to do, when you want to do it, using the WorkNow app for Android and iPhone.

These guidelines outline rules and expectations that will make sure you’re successful finding shifts, impressing coworkers and clients, getting paid, and building a reputation for excellence that increases your future opportunities.

Values / Expectations

The following values are an important part of being a member of the Shiftgig On Demand team:

Values

  1. Respect: we treat each other with respect, no matter what role we play or job we have
  2. Teamwork: we are team-driven and helpful. We make every effort to assist our coworkers on shifts so that we all perform our very best
  3. Passion: we are passionate about providing great service and wowing clients

Our clients work with us because we are the best, and we’ve earned that reputation by living up to the following expectations:

Expectations

  1. Punctual: clients are counting on us and we show up on time, every time
  2. Professional: we are trained professionals and strive to deliver excellent service at all times, including dressing appropriately and following any other shift-specific equipment requirements
  3. Experienced: we know our business inside out and can be relied upon to follow directions and perform our responsibilities; when we don’t know something, we have the good judgement to ask a supervisor
  4. Service oriented: we are customer service experts—we love working with people and providing great service to clients

How it works

  1. Find and claim shifts using the WorkNow mobile app for Android (download) or iPhone (download)
    1. You'll see shifts based on your skills and availability
    2. Some shifts are one-time events, some are repeating shifts on multiple days (and more regular income!), so be sure you read the description carefully
    3. Confirm that you meet all shift requirements including transportation and uniform
    4. Claim the shift or multiple shifts
    5. If you have to drop a shift, be sure to follow the rules for dropping shifts below
  2. Work shifts
    1. Sign in and out with your onsite contact or the Shiftgig On Demand field manager, if one is available for your shift
    2. Report your hours worked via the mobile timecard in the WorkNow app and confirm them before you leave with the onsite contact or field manager, if one is available for your shift
  3. Get paid
    1. Get paid bi-weekly, every other Friday
      1. Faster: by direct deposit through Paylocity. View your income statements online with 24/7 access to all hours worked, pay rates, and tax information
      2. Less fast: by check. Pick it up in person, or make sure we have your correct mailing address to avoid unnecessary delays

Handling Problems

Dropping shifts

If you need to drop a shift you’re scheduled to work:

Use WorkNow to drop shifts with plenty of notice. If your shifts starts soon—fewer than 48 hours before an individual shift starts or less than 1 week before a repeat shift commitment starts—you must call and speak to someone directly.

Running late

If you’re running late, be sure to contact us immediately. Remember, people are counting on you!

Disciplinary actions

Failure to follow these rules has consequences. We understand everyone has emergencies, but we must maintain certain non-negotiable guidelines for dropping shifts since people are relying on us.

  1. If you no call, no show, we’ll have to let you go. It is grounds for immediate termination.
  2. We follow a general “Three Strikes and You’re Out” rule for the following (but reserve the right to take any appropriate disciplinary action up to and including termination):
    1. Repeated call-offs which leave us in a bind to find coverage for your shift
    2. Negative client reviews (depending on severity)
    3. Reported incidents on shifts
    4. Showing up in the wrong uniform
    5. Lateness

If you have an issue with a client you believe is treating you illegally or unethically—or asking you to do something dangerous / failing to provide a safe work environment—please contact us immediately.
