Information Collected

Shiftgig On Demand's mission is to connect temporary workers with temporary job shifts. We provide no assurance of any interviews or hiring. Shiftgig On Demand users voluntarily provide information which Shiftgig On Demand uses to further its mission and provide the best possible services to its users. Some of the information collected may be personally identifiable, including names, addresses, phone numbers, and email addresses. Should you have any concerns related to providing information to us or having such information presented on the Site, please do not become a member of Shiftgig On Demand or, if applicable, close any existing accounts. Please note that, should you choose not to provide us with certain identifying information, you may be unable to use certain features of the Site. Pursuant to this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use and subject to any exclusions stated herein, you agree that all information provided to Shiftgig On Demand shall immediately become the property of Shiftgig On Demand. Shiftgig On Demand collects information from users as follows:

  1. Account Creation: In order to create an account, you must provide us with personal information such as your full name, email address, address, telephone number and a password for your account. You may be required to submit credit card information if you elect to utilize any of the services which require payment. In addition to personal information, Shiftgig On Demand will require some non-personal information in order to provide more customized services to you including employment opportunities and valuable ads. Please do not use a password that is readily ascertainable by others. By creating a Shiftgig On Demand account, you acknowledge that Shiftgig On Demand and others are allowed to use your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
  2. Profile: After creating an account, you may create a Shiftgig On Demand profile on our WorkNow app, to which you may provide additional information including corporate logos, job availability, job skills, education, professional experiences, reference letters, personal information, etc. Connecting temporary workers with temporary jobs is an important aspect of Shiftgig On Demand's services, and thus any information users provide may be used to further professional opportunities. Any information included in your profile may appear on other websites or when searches are conducted related to the subject of any of your profile information. Such profile information is publicly-viewable and may be collected and used by others. We do not control what others do with information you have posted. Therefore, we recommend that you only add information to your profile using caution and care. You expressly authorize us to use, forward, or post your profile and/or resume on other social media and internet platforms, sites or services. Further, you expressly authorize Shiftgig On Demand to use your corporate logo and corporate name, if applicable, for any promotional purposes including marketing and advertising (opt-out by e-mailing us at cancel@shiftgig.com). Any additional information you provide in order to develop your profile is completely voluntary and may be used by Shiftgig On Demand or others in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
  3. Reviews: WorkNow allows businesses to review workers. All information disclosed in these reviews is not disclosed publicly. Shiftgig On Demand expressly disclaims all liability arising from the use of information provided by users in these forums. You agree to hold Shiftgig On Demand harmless from any liability arising from your providing any information whatsoever to these forums.
  4. Information about Others: We may collect and retain any personal information provided by users about other individuals including, but not limited to, names, addresses, telephone numbers, and email addresses. Whenever you share any personal information regarding other individuals, you represent that you have the authority to do so. This information may be used by Shiftgig On Demand in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
  5. Contacts Information: Whenever you connect with other users via Shiftgig On Demand or invite new users to Shiftgig On Demand or WorkNow, information regarding that connection may be used by us to provide you with information to expand your network. All email addresses used to connect or invite users will be used to send invitations and reminders as to such invitations and the recipients of such invitations will have access to your email address. This information may be used by Shiftgig On Demand in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
  6. Automatic Transmission: Your browser automatically transmits anonymous information to every website you visit on the Internet including your IP address, information regarding the website you were on previously, etc. Shiftgig On Demand records and retains this information in order to improve upon our services and better serve our users. This information may be used by Shiftgig On Demand in accordance with this Privacy Policy and the Terms of Use.
  7. Cookies: Like most websites, we use cookies to collect anonymous information about our users and track the manner in which our users utilize the Site. Cookies are very small data files that inhabit your computer or other devices allowing us to identify users each time they return to the Site; however, cookies do not allow us to gather personally identifying information. Cookies may be controlled via the settings on your computer or other device; however, limiting the use of cookies may hinder your use of our Site. The use of cookies is done to improve the quality of our service and create a customized, personable experience for each of our users. While our primary goal regarding the use of cookies is to provide a unique experience for our users, we may also use cookies to estimate target audiences for our advertisers and track the number of people who visit certain areas of the Site and enter sweepstakes and contests. All information gathered from cookies and provided to third parties is not personally identifiable.
  8. Advertising Technology: We may use ad technology such as web beacons (a small, often-transparent graphic image that is used to monitor the behavior of a user visiting a website (also referred to as web bugs, pixel tags or clear GIFs) to track your use of our Site and to customize the advertisements that are provided to you both on and off of the Site as well as to provide research to advertisers. We use these technologies to better deliver targeted advertisements to you and to evaluate the effectiveness of advertising.
  9. Third Party Collection: Please be aware that we may allow third-parties to advertise on our Site and these companies may use tracking technologies to collect information about any users who interact with their advertisements which allows them to further deliver and measure the effectiveness of targeted advertisements. We do not provide personal information to third parties.
